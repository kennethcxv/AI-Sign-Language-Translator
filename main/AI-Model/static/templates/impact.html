Enhanced Communication Accessibility

Hand Companion addresses communication barriers for individuals with speech impairments, deafness, or other communication challenges. It provides a means to convey messages effectively through hand gestures, enabling greater inclusivity in social and professional interactions.

Real-Time Transcription

This AI technology offers real-time transcription of hand movements into written words. It helps bridge the communication gap between those who use American Sign Language (ASL) or other manual communication methods and those who rely on spoken language.

Text-to-Speech Integration

Hand Companion's integration of text-to-speech functionality allows for spoken communication based on the transcribed text. This feature not only assists the user in conveying messages but also enables them to participate in verbal conversations more readily.

Versatile Assistive Tool

Hand Companion serves as a versatile assistive tool, supporting a wide range of users, including those with disabilities and those interested in learning or using sign language. It promotes accessibility and inclusivity across various settings, from educational institutions to workplaces and daily social interactions.
